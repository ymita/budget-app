Budget

by Infragistics

Budget is a financial app for managing the budget of your organization and track incoming/outgoing payments. You can set a total budget amount and create budgets for salaries, marketing promotions, facilities etc. and define the frequency of use for these e.g., you pay salaries every month, but you may have marketing campaigns that run and are billed once a quarter. Once a budget is created and money is allocated to it you can make payments out of it until it runs out – if you overuse it, it will show up in red to alert you about overspending. Each completed financial operation creates a transaction and certain types of transactions can be verified e.g. you may issue a payment to a consultant and once you transfer the money you can mark it as verified. Budget allows you to set some global preferences related to your account credentials, notifications and currency and time zone settings.
